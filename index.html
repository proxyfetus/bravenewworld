The artist paints the participants as "embryos."
Embryo is the primitive form of us; we grow from them. Although we usually have no memories of that stage, is there a connection between what's me now and then? The artist intends to reach to that stage in retrospect through stories of life, as well as discuss the transitionality and eternity of human beings as a life form. The theme also touches upon the spirituality, the ethicist of abortion, IVF and artificial intelligence.
